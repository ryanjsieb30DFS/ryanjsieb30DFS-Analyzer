## Physical profile

- Otherwise a roughly neutral run environment — the edge here is certainty, not

## Strategic notes (one slate)

- The roof's value is RELATIVE: it scales with how much weather risk sits on the
  rest of the slate. On a clean slate it's just a neutral park; on a rain-heavy
  slate it holds the only guaranteed 27 outs, and the field systematically
  underprices that.
