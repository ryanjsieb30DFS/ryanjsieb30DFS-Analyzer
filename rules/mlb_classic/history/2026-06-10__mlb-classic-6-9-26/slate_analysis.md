## Anchor-Equivalence check (mandatory)

Three equivalence classes flagged, and they collapse into two real decisions:

1. **Pitcher anchors** — Burns / Cease / Peralta / May / Teng all within 23.6–19.4% (May 28.8%). The field treats them as interchangeable. **Alternative anchor: Wheeler (9.5%).** With only one unique lineup, satisfying the rule means *not* defaulting to the most popular member of the class — run Wheeler or Cease (the lower-owned premium pair) over Burns/May if conviction is otherwise equal.
2. **Stack anchors** — MIL / CHC / ATH at 86–101% combined own are equivalent anchors. **Alternative: ATL** (equal-or-better hitter form, second-best implied total, ~1/3 the ownership). The single lineup should anchor on at most one mega-chalk stack and take the alternative on the other slot.
3. The hitter-tier flags (Langeliers/Bauers/Turang; PCA/Hoerner/Chourio/Rooker; Happ/Busch/Yelich/etc.) are all downstream of decision #2 — resolving the stack choice resolves them.
