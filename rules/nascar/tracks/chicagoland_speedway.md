## Track profile
- **1.52-mile tri-oval intermediate**, D-shaped configuration, **18° banking** in the turns.
- **Notable tire degradation / high tire wear** — the defining trait. Comparable tire-wear tracks: Darlington, Homestead. Comparable banking/shape: Kansas, Las Vegas (D-shaped intermediates).
- Multiple grooves exist (room to race side-by-side).
- **Treacherous surface**: patches and seams over bumps in the corners; a pit-road entry that "sneaks up" off turn 4 with a sharp exit before the commitment line → expect **pit-road penalties / missed stops** (a source of chaos + PD).
- Because of high tire wear, expect **comers-and-goers with fast laps spread throughout the field as drivers pit off-cycle** — raises upside for the whole field but makes fast-lap distribution unpredictable (article: AJ Allmendinger scored 29 fast laps at Darlington this year without leading a lap).

## Intermediate optimal-lineup profile (2026 season, from DFR reference tables)
Across the 6 intermediate optimals so far this season (Nashville, Charlotte, Texas, Kansas, Darlington, Las Vegas):
- **Every optimal except Texas rostered ≥3 drivers starting top 15.** More laps than lower series → more dominator points → front-runners with little PD upside still hit.
- **A front-two-rows starter (P4 or better) with double-digit laps led was in every posted optimal**, usually at $9,500+; all but Darlington featured multiple $10,000+ drivers → elite high-priced teams are strong here.
- **All 6 optimals rostered multiple JGR/23XI Toyotas** — clearly the best org on this track type in 2026.
- **Value (<$6,000) optimal appearances** started 11th, 22nd, 26th, 30th, 18th, 19th, 22nd, 15th, 30th → scores 30–53 fpts. **4 of 6 optimals featured 2 sub-$6,000 drivers** → punting is viable if the punt can sniff 30 points.
- Starting-position → Top-10 DK score distribution (DFR): Top 5 = 28.3%, Top 10 = 43.3%, 11–20 = 25.0%, 21–30 = 13.3%, worse-than-30th = 18.3%. Optimal-lineup share: Top 5 = 27.8%, Top 10 = 36.1%, 11–20 = 30.6%, 21–30 = 25.0%, worse-than-30th = 8.3%.
