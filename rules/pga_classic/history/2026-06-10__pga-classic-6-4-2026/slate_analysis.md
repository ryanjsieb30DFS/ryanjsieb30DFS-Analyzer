## The course read (drives the whole build)

Muirfield is **one of the hardest non-major tests on Tour** (~1.2 over par adjusted since 2015). Brutal missed-fairway penalty (top-5 on Tour), GIR near the bottom 15%, Augusta-like firm/fast greens. **The winner is an elite ball-striker tee-to-green who avoids the big number** — "limit the damage, let the field beat itself."

- **ETR course-fit lean:** total driving (accuracy-weighted) + approach + around-the-green. Boosts: **Henley #1, Scottie #2, M.Fitzpatrick / Fleetwood / Si Woo #3, A.Fitzpatrick #6, Aaron Rai / Nick Taylor #7, J.J. Spaun #9, Aberg #10.** Tom Hoge is the #1 fit *dock*.
- **Course-history stickiness = 0.42 at Muirfield** — clearly above-average predictive (vs. Aronimink 0.11 last week, Quail 0.48). **Course history carries real weight here**, which elevates the CH-boost darts below.
- Muted scoring → position points matter more as a % of the win → **winning the tournament matters more** → strengthens the Scottie argument (2× defending champ).
