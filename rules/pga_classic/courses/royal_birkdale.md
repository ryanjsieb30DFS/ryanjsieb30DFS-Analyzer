## Course profile
- Par 70, 7,223 yards (up only 67 yds from 2017). 11th Open here, first since 2017 (Spieth -12 over Kuchar; 21 players under par; Branden Grace's 62 — still the only one in an Open).
- Full Mackenzie & Ebert redesign begun May 2024 — every hole touched: new drivable 321-yd 5th, rebuilt 151-yd 7th, par-5 14th stretched to 602 into the prevailing wind (12 bunkers), brand-new 241-yd par-3 15th, 16th shortened to 393, 18th tee consolidated with six bunkers at 330–350 driver range. Par 3s now 151/186/219/241. Pre-2017 course history is of limited value after the redesign.
- Fescue fairways/tees, small (~5,200 sq ft) fescue-browntop bent greens, 108 revetted pot bunkers, none behind a green. Flat fairways in dune valleys, almost nothing blind — "the most straightforward course on the rota," but constant direction changes vs the wind. Back nine 3,829 yds vs front 3,394.
- ETR: pot bunkers off the tee are effectively one-stroke penalties; tee-shot decision-making (drivable 5th, reachable par 5s, 18th) is the winning skill.
