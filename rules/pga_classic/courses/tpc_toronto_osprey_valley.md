# TPC Toronto at Osprey Valley — North Course

**UNVERIFIED — built from this slate's articles only** (RBC Canadian Open 6.11.26 slate data: ETR Course Fits + Stickiness articles). Verify against future slates before treating as ground truth.

## Layout
- Par 70, 7,389 yards — two par 5s (reachable 1st, 581-yard 18th), four par 3s, twelve par 4s
- Bent/poa greens, tees, fairways; thick bluegrass rough at ~3.75"
- Several long par 4s (530-yd 17th, 526-yd 13th, 513-yd 16th)

## How it plays (one Tour year of data — 2025 RBC Canadian Open)
- Easy: ~1.6 strokes under par adjusted; among the ~15 easiest venues ETR tracks. Birdie-fest.
- Par 5s (1 and 18) ranked third-easiest set on Tour — getting after them is near-mandatory
- Wide fairways (39+ yds, top-15 on Tour), very few penalties, top-15 adjusted driving distance — green light for driver
- GIR ~73% (top-10 on Tour) — lots of mid-range birdie looks
- BUT missed-fairway penalty top-25% on Tour: the 3.75" bluegrass rough is the course's only defense
- 2025: SG:APP explained ~40% of scoring variance (Tour avg ~35%), inflated by a few blowup rounds; ARG explained just ~6% (less than half Tour avg ~14%) — short game barely mattered
- ETR's conclusion after clipping outliers: profiles as a fairly AVERAGE test; light course-fit adjustments only, tilting modestly toward accuracy off the tee and away from distance
- Course-history stickiness: **0.06** — one of the least CH-predictive courses on the 2026 list. Course history is noise here.

## Weather pattern
- 2025 and 2026 both: heavy rain pre-tournament, scattered storms — soft and receptive, fire-at-flags conditions that emphasize length somewhat, but the wet rough makes the accuracy/distance tradeoff balanced
