## The Classic-vs-RD4-SD leverage gap (the key cross-format finding)
| | Winners avg own | Winners sub-10% (median) | Won w/ ≥2 sub-10% |
|---|---|---|---|
| **PGA Classic** | 12.9% | 2.5 | 78% |
| **PGA RD4 SD** | 19.1% | 1.7 | 64% |
RD4 SD winners are **~6 ownership points chalkier** and run **~1 fewer dart** than Classic
winners. Mechanism: a single final round (vs 2 days + cut), smaller fields, and lower
scoring variance compress the edge of going contrarian — leverage is still required, but
in a smaller dose. **Do not import Classic's contrarian dosage into RD4 SD.**
